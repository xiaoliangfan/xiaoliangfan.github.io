Biography

Xiaoliang Fan is a Senior Research Specialist at Fujian Key Laboratory of Sensing and Computing for Smart Cites, School of Informatics, Xiamen University, China. He received his PhD degree at University Pierre and Marie CURIE, France in 2012. His research interests include Trustworthy AI and federated learning, Spatio-temporal data mining, Large model applications, etc. He has published 80+ journals (IEEE TSC/TMC/TITS, etc.) and top conferences (AAAI, KDD, IJCAI, WWW, etc.) papers. His works are funded by NSFC and many industry collaborators. Dr. FAN is ACM/IEEE/CCF Senior Member.
